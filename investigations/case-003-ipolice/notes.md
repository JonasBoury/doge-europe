## Working Hypotheses

1. **Scope creep hypothesis:** The iPolice program suffered from continuously expanding requirements, driven by the complexity of 185+ police zones with varying needs.

2. **Procurement failure hypothesis:** The vendor selection process prioritized established relationships over technical capability and competitive pricing.

3. **Governance gap hypothesis:** No single authority had sufficient mandate and expertise to steer a program of this scale across federal and local police.
